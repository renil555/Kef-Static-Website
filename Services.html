Services

Check our Services

Kerala Exporters’ forum will be issuing Registration cum Membership Certificate to our registered members who are eligible to attend and participate in discussion, Seminars, Colloquia, Arbitration and Expo within the Country and across the world were Kerala Exporters’ forum has been approved by the concerned Chamber/Trade bodies of the Said Country or Nation.

Kerala Exporters’ forum will organise events and Conferences with the support of DGFT, RBI, FOREX Banks, FICCI & FIEO to enhance trade related programs, Forum conducted Sessions on ODOP, Rupee Trading Mechanism on International Trade, aggressive increase in Ocean and Air freight, GST on Export freight etc for the last several months. Our member fraternity can have access to get the online journal Portal issued by FIEO, FICCI other Shippng Lines and Airlines and daily updates on Vessels Schedules from Cochin to various destinations.


Kerala Exporters’ forum issues COO Certificate to our member community upon the submission of Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading and Shipping Bill Copies to be able to submit their Consignee in their respective Countries/Customs Departmnt of the Concerned to clear their Cargo.

Arrangements will be made to support International Expo and B2B meetings overseas to work for trade enhancement of Our existing Customer base to get a more Customer base overseas.


Kerala Exporters’ forum issues various trade related journals and periodicals with the help of FIEO, FICCI, Shipping and Airlines and Air and Sea Ports their respective monthly and quarterly trade through respective Air and Sea Ports. Forum publishes this quarterly/fortnightly and almost all Media’s including Print Media and Channels covers this and some of the publications will be available through Shipping Ministry websites.


Forum on various occasions alerts its member community regarding various Policy measures taken by DGFT, Central Banks, Customs, and other Partner Government Agencies both from State and Central Government. These includes duty free Credit Scrip, Drawbk Collection, RODTP & ROTSL & Coastal Navigation incentives etc. Forum also helps our member fraternity to achieve minimal expense on export by earning these incentives


Kerala Exporters’ forum will publish through emails and WhatsApp, Twitter and Facebook communication platform the queries will be lodged through these channels apart from kef websites. Members as well as non members can avail these facilities free of cost.

Kerala Exporters’ forum for its members had lodged a self lodging complaint solution network through online grievance redressal mechanism to the respective agencies for the failure in Shipping their export Cargo. Kerala Exporters’ forum will take up the concerns to Banks/Shipping and Airlines/ FIEO, and Ministry of Commerce/Finance to enhance smooth clearance.
